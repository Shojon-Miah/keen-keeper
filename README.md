# Keen-Keeper

**KeenKeeper is a personal relationship management app that helps you stay connected with the people who matter most in your life.**

## Technologies Used

- **Next.js** (App Router)
- **Tailwind CSS** + **DaisyUI**
- **Recharts**
- **React Icons**

## Key Features

**Friend Tracking** — Keep track of all your friends with their contact history, status, and tags.

**Quick Check-In** — Log calls, texts, and video interactions directly from a friend's detail page.

**Friendship Analytics** — Visualize your interaction history with a pie chart showing Call, Text, and Video breakdowns.
